A recent post by Progrium (aka webhook’s biggest evangelist) reminded me that I needed to show how easy it was to plug Superfeedr in a network of webhooks.Here is what you can do, quite quickly!Piping One of the great things with webhooks is that they’re litterally just HTTP urls that can run some arbitrary code.Once you understand that a given hook can also call another hook, it’s obvious to think that you can chain multiple hooks to reach the result you want.For example, you could have a webhook that converts some data into some other data before calling another hook.
