Federal–compatible Terms of Service (TOS) agreements are special agreements negotiated between the federal government and vendors who offer free social media tools.These federal–compatible TOS agreements modify or remove problematic clauses in standard TOS agreements, and allow federal employees to legally use these tools.Why Its Important Social Media is key to helping federal agencies engage with their customers and accomplish their mission. However, the standard Terms of Service (or Terms of Use) for most free social media products are incompatible with federal law, regulation, or practice.  To meet this need, a coalition of federal agencies, led by GSA, works with a broad range of providers of no-cost social media products to develop amended, federally compatible TOS agreements.
